About the release

ABOUT

PLURAL is a high-energy Afro-house release built around heavy rhythm, electronic textures, and a bold 3-step bounce. The project carries a futuristic feel while staying rooted in deep African groove.

Driven by powerful drums, thick bass movement, sharp synth work, and an intense dancefloor atmosphere, PLURAL captures motion, contrast, and underground energy.
